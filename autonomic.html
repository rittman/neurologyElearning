Autonomic symptoms

Abnormalities of the autonomic nervous system appear many years before the onset of Parkinson's disease.

The main autonomic symptoms to keep in mind are. Click for details on how to manage each symptom.

Early satiety

Smaller portion sizes, eating little and often

Changes in sweating

Use mild soap and antiperspirant. Avoid hot rooms, spicy food and tight-fitting synethetic clothing

Erectile dysfunction

Medications such as Sildenafil (viagra) can be helpful

Postural hypotension

Non-pharmacological measures include

  • drinking plenty of fluids
  • pressure stockings
  • taking time to sit before standing up from lying down

Pharmacological measures include

  • reducing the levodopa dose
  • fludrocortisone
  • midodrine
